Shrek
Shrek, the grumpy Ogre, lives alone in his beloved home, the stinking swamp. But one morning, a fairy-tale essence overwhelms his empire. Furious, the Ogre visits the lord to reclaim the peace of his home. But first you have to free the beautiful princess Fiona from the depths of a castle where she is protected by a fire-breathing dragon so that Farquaad can marry her and thus become king. During the exciting adventures, Donkey will be Shrek's companion, who sometimes unintentionally hinders and does not help the Ogre's situation.
